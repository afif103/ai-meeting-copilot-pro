# Create Your Own Persona

This guide helps you create a personalized AI prompt for the Meeting Copilot app.

---

## How It Works

1. Copy the template below
2. Paste it into **ChatGPT** or **Claude**
3. Fill in YOUR information
4. Get your personalized prompt
5. Paste the prompt into the app (click "Edit" button → paste → Save)

---

## Step 1: Copy This Template

Copy everything in the box below and paste it to ChatGPT or Claude:

```
I need you to create a personalized AI prompt for a meeting assistant app. The app listens to conversations and suggests what I should say in response.

Here's my information:

=== ABOUT ME ===
Name: [Your name]
Age: [Your age]
Current Job: [Your current job or "looking for work"]
Target Job: [Job you're applying for, or "N/A"]
Years of Experience: [How many years in your field]

=== MY BACKGROUND ===
[Write 3-5 sentences about your career background, education, and key experiences]

=== MY SKILLS ===
[List your top 5-10 skills, separated by commas]

=== MY PROJECTS/ACHIEVEMENTS ===
[List 2-3 projects or achievements you're proud of. Include specific numbers if possible]

=== WHAT I NEED HELP WITH ===
[Choose one or more:]
- Job interviews
- Sales calls
- Customer service
- Technical meetings
- General conversations
- English practice
- Other: [specify]

=== MY COMMUNICATION STYLE ===
[Choose words that describe how you want to sound:]
- Professional / Casual
- Confident / Humble
- Direct / Diplomatic
- Technical / Simple
- Formal / Friendly

=== LANGUAGE ===
My native language: [Your native language]
I need help with: [English fluency / pronunciation / vocabulary / confidence]

=== SPECIAL REQUESTS ===
[Any specific things you want the AI to help with, or situations you face often]

---

Now create a prompt for me using this format:

1. Start with "You are [my name] answering/responding in [context]"
2. Include my background briefly
3. List my key skills and projects with specific metrics
4. Add rules for how to respond:
   - Write only what I should say (direct speech)
   - Keep responses short (1-5 sentences)
   - Match my communication style
   - Include specific examples from my experience when relevant
5. Use {snippet} where the transcript goes
6. Use {context_summary} where background context goes

Make the prompt concise but effective. It should help me respond naturally and confidently.
```

## Step 3: Get Your Prompt

After you paste the filled template to ChatGPT/Claude, it will generate a personalized prompt for you.

The prompt will look something like this:

```
You are Maria answering a Senior Frontend Developer interview. You are a confident developer with 3 years of React experience who transitioned from design.

### MY BACKGROUND
- 3 years frontend development (React, TypeScript)
- Former graphic designer - strong UI/UX sense
- AWS certified, worked at 2 startups
- Built e-commerce site handling 10,000 daily users

### MY KEY ACHIEVEMENTS
- Reduced page load time by 40%
- Led redesign that increased engagement by 25%
- Built scalable React applications

### WHAT WAS ASKED
{snippet}

### CONTEXT
{context_summary}

### RULES
- Write ONLY what I should say (direct speech)
- Be confident and specific
- Include metrics when relevant (40% faster, 10,000 users)
- Keep answers to 3-5 sentences
- If unsure: "I haven't done that specifically, but I have experience with [related thing]"
```

---

## Step 4: Use Your Prompt in the App

1. Open **AI Meeting Copilot Pro**
2. Click the **"Edit"** button (next to the persona dropdown)
3. Delete the default text
4. **Paste** your personalized prompt
5. Click **"Save & Use"**
6. Done! The AI will now respond as YOU

---

## Tips for a Good Prompt

1. **Be specific** - Include real numbers and project names
2. **Keep it concise** - The prompt shouldn't be too long (500-1000 words max)
3. **Include your style** - How do you want to sound?
4. **Add examples** - What phrases or approaches work for you?
5. **Test and adjust** - If the suggestions don't feel right, edit the prompt

## Quick Templates

### For Job Interviews
Focus on:
- Your target job title
- Your top 3 achievements with metrics
- Your failure/learning story
- Why you want this role

### For Customer Service
Focus on:
- Your company/product knowledge
- How to handle complaints
- Your tone (friendly, professional, empathetic)
- Common scenarios you face

### For Sales Calls
Focus on:
- Your product benefits
- Handling objections
- Your closing style
- Building rapport

### For Technical Meetings
Focus on:
- Your technical expertise
- How you explain complex topics
- Your problem-solving approach
- Tools and technologies you know
